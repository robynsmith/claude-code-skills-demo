## Entry Format

```markdown
### [Day of Week], [Month] [Day] [Year]

**Remind** - Recall priorities, goals, and quests
- Current quarterly focus: [if defined]
- This week's quests were: [list]

**Reflect** - Look back and celebrate wins
- Wins this week:
  - [accomplishments, even small ones]
- Challenges:
  - [what was hard, what got stuck]
- Surprises:
  - [unexpected learnings or events]

**Plan** - Define priorities for next week
- Urgent:
  - [time-sensitive items]
- Important:
  - [high-leverage but not urgent]
- Quests for next week:
  - Work: [focus areas]
  - Personal: [focus areas]
```

## Guiding Questions

**For Remind:**
- "What were you hoping to accomplish this week?"
- "What quests were you working on?"

**For Reflect:**
- "What are you most proud of this week?"
- "What felt hard or draining?"
- "What surprised you?"
- "What patterns do you notice?"

**For Plan:**
- "What absolutely must happen next week?"
- "What would make next week feel successful?"
- "What are you avoiding that needs attention?"
